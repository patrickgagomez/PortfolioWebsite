Introduction

My name is Patrick Gomez, and I am a Computer Scientist, Game Designer, and 3D Artist. As long as I can remember, video games have been the most important part of my life. I have always been fascinated by the magic of interactive storytelling and the power of games to evoke emotions and create unique experiences.

This entire website was built by hand using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I hope you enjoy your stay!



Education

I graduated from University of California: Santa Cruz with a BA in Computer Science and Game Design. During my time at UCSC, I had the opportunity to work on various projects, honing my skills and gaining invaluable experience.



Passion for Game Design

I am passionate about creating games that are fun, immersive, and memorable. I believe that games are a powerful medium that can be used to tell stories, teach, and inspire.



Skills

My expertise lies in creating 3D art and game assets that transport players to extraordinary worlds. Additionally, I am proficient in several programming languages [C# / C++, Python, Lua, Javascript, HTML & CSS], and have hands-on experience with popular game development engines like Unreal Engine, Unity, Godot, Pico8, Construct3 and even Twine!
