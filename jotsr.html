Roles: Game Artist, Game Programmer

"Journey on the Sea Railway" is an enchanting and immersive 3D puzzle-adventure game inspired by Studio Ghibli's acclaimed film "Spirited Away." In this captivating experience, players will embark on a mesmerizing journey aboard the mystical train that traverses an ocean-like expanse, encountering unforgettable characters, solving intricate puzzles, and discovering the true meaning of courage, friendship, and self-discovery.

As players delve into the heart of the "Journey on the Sea Railway," they will uncover a multi-layered narrative that explores the delicate balance between the human world and the realm of spirits. The train itself becomes a symbolic bridge connecting these two realms, each carriage representing a unique facet of the human experience and the emotional struggles faced by the spirits.

Through thought-provoking encounters with spirits seeking solace and resolution, players will be prompted to reflect on their understanding of empathy, acceptance, and the importance of preserving the natural order.

Story and Setting

The game follows the story of Chihiro, a brave young girl who finds herself lost in a strange world filled with spirits, gods, and magic. As the story unfolds, Chihiro encounters a mysterious train that rides across the vast ocean, carrying both familiar and unknown passengers. Drawn by an inexplicable force, Chihiro boards the train and must now navigate its secrets, challenges, and peculiar inhabitants to find her way back home.

Narrative and Theme

At its core, "Journey on the Sea Railway" embraces themes of courage, self-discovery, and the power of human connections. is set in a wondrous and ethereal world that seamlessly blends reality with the mystical. The primary setting is a vast, ocean-like train that sails across an otherworldly expanse, taking passengers on a surreal and transformative journey. Inside the train, each compartment holds its unique charm, presenting visually striking environments inspired by Studio Ghibli's artistry.
