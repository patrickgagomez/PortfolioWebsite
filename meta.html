Roles: Game Artist, Game Programmer

Metamorphoverse is an exhilarating and innovative top-down spleef arcade-style shooter game that takes players on an extraordinary adventure through the vast expanse of reality as a verse-jumping insect. Embrace your metamorphic powers and traverse four distinct realities, each brimming with unique art styles, challenges, and adversaries. As players embark on this captivating journey, they will encounter breathtaking landscapes, solve mind-bending puzzles, and engage in intense shoot-out battles, all while uncovering the secrets of the interconnected multiverse.

Download Game

Verse-Jumping

Assume the role of a dynamic verse-jumping insect, capable of shifting seamlessly between four different realities. Each realm will present a myriad of obstacles, enemies, and environmental hazards, calling for quick reflexes, strategic thinking, and adaptability.

Distinct Art Styles

Immerse yourself in four enchanting and visually distinct art styles, each representing a different reality within the Metamorphoverse. From the disgusting, life-like textures of the real world, to the softness of the "Sketchy" paper and pencil dimension, there is much to explore!
