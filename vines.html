Roles: Game Artist, Game Programmer

In "Vines," players embark on an action-packed adventure set in a world overtaken by nature's fury. The once-prosperous city has been engulfed by an unstoppable overgrowth of technologically enhanced plant monsters, threatening to wipe out all traces of human civilization. Players assume the role of an intrepid protagonist, a skilled adventurer armed with a powerful punching glove imbued with ancient, long-lost technology.

Encounter the ultimate challenge: a colossal, ancient plant monster, infused with the malevolent energy of the rogue scientist's experiments. This towering monstrosity is the source of the city's overgrowth and the epicenter of the plant monsters' hive mind. The showdown with this formidable boss will test your platforming skills, combat prowess, and strategic thinking like never before.

Vertical Platforming and Exploration

Navigate through multi-layered, vertically designed levels, blending natural landscapes with remnants of a once-thriving metropolis. Swing on vines, scale towering structures, and leap between platforms as you unveil hidden secrets and restore the city's forgotten history.

Art Direction

Experience a visually stunning game world with lush vegetation, contrasting against the remnants of human technology. The art style seamlessly blends nature and machinery, creating an immersive and captivating atmosphere.
