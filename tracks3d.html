Roles: Game Designer, Game Artist, Game Producer

"Tracks" personifies the idea of the seven sins of mankind and extends the fight against true moral dilemmas to a more personal level for the player. The game’s protagonist is a train conductor, piloting an unusual train. Upon closer inspection, he finds his passengers might not be who they say they are… Set forth in this stylized 3d first-person shooter game and blast through enemies!

As our protagonist embarks on this extraordinary journey, little does he know that his seemingly routine passengers are concealing dark secrets that challenge the very fabric of their souls. As he delves deeper into the intricacies of their identities, a thrilling narrative unfolds, blurring the lines between good and evil, right and wrong.

The lush and stylized 3D world of "Tracks" welcomes players into an intricately designed first-person shooter game, where every detail is meticulously crafted to immerse you in a visually stunning and atmospheric environment. Engage in heart-pounding battles, honing your shooting skills, and strategizing your approach to overcome hordes of formidable enemies.

Concept

"Tracks" takes after its predecessors (Bioshock, Dishonored, etc.), making the game a linear, story based shooter that includes abilities. Each train car is meant to embody a different deadly sin and each train car employs some new mechanic or bullet type. Players must scrounge each train car for ammo and health, and attempt to rid the train of any trespassers.

Theme and Setting

The story takes place in a fantasy version of the Wild West in which augmentations and technology are more prevalent and wicked. The player gets to explore different train cars, fighting through enemies and gaining new allies.
