Roles: Game Designer, Game 3D Artist

In this unique adventure puzzle game, you take on the role of a courageous protagonist, a lone explorer seeking answers and revelations amidst the juxtaposition of industrial remnants and vibrant plant life. The eerily captivating atmosphere of the game will draw you into a forgotten world where mystery and enchantment intertwine.

This game features a visually stunning 3D world crafted with 2D assets, creating an immersive environment filled with intricate details. As you venture deeper into the heart of the ruins, you will encounter awe-inspiring landscapes, overgrown corridors, and hauntingly beautiful chambers waiting to be explored.

Art Direction and Stylistic Choice

We developed a unique art style that combines the depth of a 3D world with the charm and simplicity of 2D assets. This captured the essence of this atmospheric setting through captivating visuals perfectly.

Engaging Platforming Puzzles

Challenge players with a variety of platforming puzzles that test their wit, agility, and strategic thinking. Each level will present new and exciting obstacles, pushing players to utilize their interchangeable limbs to overcome each trial. Players collect and swap out replacement limbs, each with distinct platforming abilities.
