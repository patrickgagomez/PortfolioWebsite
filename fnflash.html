Roles: Game Programmer

A unique and innovative take on the wildly popular battle royale game, Fortnite. This version brings the excitement of the original game to your browser, but with a twist! Instead of flashy graphics and complex mechanics, "Fortnite: Flash" utilizes data, random generation, and quick time events to deliver an immersive text-based gaming experience.

The objective remains the same: be the last player standing. However, in this version, you'll need to make strategic decisions by carefully analyzing data and statistics. As you explore the text-based environment, you'll encounter various locations and engage in intense battles through descriptive text-based encounters. Use your wits to outsmart opponents and secure victory.

Concepting and Creating

The game follows was actually made in p5.js as a "creative coding" exersize. "Fortnite: Flash" uses real world data from the actual game of Fortnite to place it's loot and calculate gameplay.

Replayability

With dynamic world generation and random events, no two matches are ever the same! Prepare for infinite replayability as you explore new terrains, face unpredictable challenges, and devise unique strategies with every playthrough.
